Yesterday, I had an embarrassing moment at the gym. I was lifting with a friend.
We had just wrapped up our final set when he leapt off the bench and started throwing weights.

"What are you doing?" I asked, looking around.
"Superset bro. Let us do this."

Look, I am not gonna lie. I used to believe supersets were magic too.
But after 8 years of training, I learned the hard way.
The real secret? Consistency and progressive overload. That is it.

My friend Dave told me about this last Saturday over coffee.
"You do not need complicated programs," he said. "You just need to show up."
